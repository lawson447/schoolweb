Language Arts

The Language Arts curriculum at our school emphasizes the development of reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. Students read a variety of literature, including fiction and non-fiction texts, and write for a variety of purposes and audiences. The curriculum also includes grammar instruction and vocabulary development.

Grade Level Reading Writing Speaking and Listening
Kindergarten Develop phonemic awareness and phonics skills; read and comprehend text Write short narratives and informative texts; use proper capitalization, punctuation, and spelling Participate in class discussions and oral presentations
1st Grade Read and comprehend fiction and non-fiction texts; develop fluency Write simple narratives, explanations, and opinions; use sentence structure and conventions Listen actively in class discussions and follow oral directions
2nd Grade Read and comprehend grade-level texts with accuracy and fluency; make inferences and connections Write informative, explanatory, and narrative texts with clear topics and details; revise and edit for conventions and style Participate in collaborative conversations and respond thoughtfully to others' ideas

Mathematics

The Mathematics curriculum at our school develops students' understanding of concepts and skills related to numbers, operations, geometry, measurement, and data analysis. Students engage in problem-solving activities and develop reasoning and critical thinking skills.

Grade Level Numbers and Operations Geometry and Measurement Data Analysis and Probability
Kindergarten Understand numbers 1-10; develop counting skills Identify and describe 2D and 3D shapes; understand spatial relationships Collect and organize data; understand chance and randomness
1st Grade Add and subtract within 20; understand place value Identify and describe 2D and 3D shapes; understand fractions and partitioning Collect and record data; understand probability
2nd Grade Add and subtract within 100; understand place value Identify and describe 2D and 3D shapes; understand symmetry and congruence Collect and represent data using graphs; understand likelihood and outcomes

Science

The Science curriculum at our school focuses on inquiry-based learning, where students explore and investigate scientific concepts and ideas. Students learn about physical, earth, and life sciences, as well as scientific inquiry and investigation.

Grade Level Physical Science Earth Science Life Science Scientific Inquiry and Investigation
Kindergarten Understand the properties of objects and materials; explore motion and forces Observe and describe weather and the changing seasons; understand basic Earth geology and mineral identification Explore and compare plants and animals; understand habitats and ecosystems Pose questions and make observations; plan and carry out simple investigations; communicate results
1st Grade Understand the physical properties of matter; explore sound and light Observe and describe the Moon, stars, and space; understand simple geological processes and rock identification Compare and classify different animals and their adaptations; understand basic plant structures and functions Pose questions and plan investigations; collect and analyze data; draw conclusions and communicate results
2nd Grade Understand basic energy concepts and transformations; explore matter and its changes Observe and describe the water cycle and its effect on the environment; understand basic weather patterns and climate Explore relationships between organisms and their environments; understand basic human anatomy and health Pose questions and make predictions; design and conduct investigations; analyze and interpret data; communicate findings

Curriculum

Our school curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive education to our students that prepares them for a successful future. We offer a wide range of courses, including:

  • English Language Arts
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Foreign Languages
  • Physical Education
  • Visual and Performing Arts

Our teachers are highly qualified and experienced in their respective fields, and they are committed to providing a challenging and engaging curriculum to all students.

Activities

Our school offers a variety of extracurricular activities to enrich the educational experience of our students. We believe that extracurricular activities are an important part of a well-rounded education and provide students with opportunities to develop their talents and interests.

  • Athletics, such as basketball, volleyball, and cross country
  • Music programs, including band, choir, and orchestra
  • Clubs for art, drama, debate, and more
  • Community service projects
  • Field trips and educational travel opportunities

Our extracurricular activities are led by highly qualified and dedicated teachers and coaches who are committed to helping students develop their skills and interests outside of the classroom.
