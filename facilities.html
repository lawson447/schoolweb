School Facilities

Classrooms

We have spacious and well-equipped classrooms to provide a conducive learning environment for our students.

Library

Our library is stocked with a wide range of books, references, and digital resources to support research and learning.

Playground

We have a large playground area where students can engage in physical activities and have fun during break times.

Our well-equipped science lab allows students to conduct experiments and explore the world of science hands-on.

Gymnasium

We have a fully equipped gymnasium to promote physical fitness and sports activities among our students.

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria offers a variety of nutritious meals and snacks to keep our students energized throughout the day.
