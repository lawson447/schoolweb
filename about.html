READ ABOUT BROFOYEDUR D/A BASIC SCHOOL

Brofoyedur D/A is an educational institution where students go to learn and acquire knowledge. It is a place where teachers provide instruction and guidance to students in various subjects such as math, science, history, and literature. Schools typically have classrooms, libraries, laboratories, and other facilities to support the learning process. Students attend school to gain academic skills, socialize with their peers, and prepare for their future careers.


Brofoyedur D/A School is a basic school located in Ajumako Enyan Essiam, Ghana-Central Region. It is a co-educational school that provides education for students aged 6 to 18. The school offers a wide range of subjects and has a strong focus on academic excellence

Brofoyedur School has a dedicated and experienced staff who are committed to providing high-quality education to their students. The school offers a broad curriculum that includes subjects such as English, mathematics, science, history, cpmputing, languages, and the Social Studies. They also provide opportunities for students to participate in extracurricular activities such as sports, music, drama, and clubs.
