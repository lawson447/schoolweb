Sports

Our school offers a range of sports activities to keep students active and healthy. Our sports facilities include a gymnasium, football field, basketball court and volleyball court. We also have a range of sports clubs where students can compete and develop their skills.

Music

Our school has a strong music program with a variety of musical instruments available for students to learn and play. We have a range of music clubs, including choir and band, where students can develop their musical skills and perform for the school community.

Clubs

Our school has a diverse range of clubs and activities to cater to the interests of all students. We have clubs for art, drama, chess, debate, coding and much more. These clubs provide a positive and engaging learning environment for students to explore their passions and develop new skills.

Events

Our school hosts a variety of events throughout the year to engage with the school community. These include book fairs, science fairs, art shows and talent shows. We also organize community service events to promote social responsibility and civic engagement among students.
